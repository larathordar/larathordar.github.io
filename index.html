New life in Stockholm

Moving to Stockholm has been a wonderful decision for me. It's getting darker and colder every day but the christmas lights are shining and the snow lights up the ground. In a way it's a lot like home and much quieter than my former life in London.

My local library

My local library is one of the most beautful libraries I've ever seen. It has a big round hall in the centre with books in all sorts of languages. Including Icelandic. I applied for my library card today and I can't wait to spend the cold winter months reading a book or two.
